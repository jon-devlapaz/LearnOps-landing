# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Learners who want to truly understand material, not just memorize or skim. Those who value evidence-based learning (spaced repetition, active recall) over gamification.

## Product Purpose
socratink is a metacognitive learning tool for learning by reconstruction. It provides a "truthful map" of understanding. The core promise: see what you can actually explain.

## Brand Personality
Scholarly, evidence-based, polished. Calm, precise, Socratic. Voice of a reading room and field journal.

## Anti-references
- Gamification (Duolingo, Quizlet, streaks, badges, XP)
- "AI Tutor knows your mind" framing (Khanmigo)
- Course completion/mastery platforms (Coursera)
- Hype, exclamation marks, emoji, "tailored just for you"
- Dashboard aesthetics, neon, gradients, side-stripe borders

## Design Principles
- **Evidence-respecting:** The graph stays honest because evidence comes from reconstruction, not reading.
- **Quiet exactness:** The visual register matches a reading room, not a SaaS dashboard.
- **Refuse to be cute:** The willingness to look austere instead of friendly.
- **Anti-pitch clarity:** State clearly what the tool is *not*.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast, legible typography across devices.
- Visually hidden `<h1>` for screen readers is exactly `socratink`.
- Calm, reduced motion (no rotating animations or particles).
